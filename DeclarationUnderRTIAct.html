Declaration under RTI Act

Declaration under S. 4 (1)(b) of RTI Act

Particulars Details
The particulars of the organisation A grant-in-aid higher education institution affiliated to the R.T.M. Nagpur University, offering UG and PG courses in arts, commerce, science, and management faculties.
The powers & duties of its officers and employees Principal: Overall management and administration, monitoring, planning, and implementation.
Teaching Faculty: Teaching, assessment, professional development, contribution to responsibilities of the college.
Office Staff: Managing administration, student services, staff records and procedures, accounting, and reporting.
Library Staff: Managing library including e-resources, maintenance of records, handling transactions, procurement, and management of stocks.
Laboratory Staff: Supporting laboratory work of teachers and students, maintenance of laboratories.
The procedure followed in the decision-making process Primary decision-making authority is the Principal, who works within the norms of affiliating university, state government, UGC, and management trust. Various committees and cells involving staff handle decision-making and planning.
Norms set for discharge of its functions Norms are set by the UGC, the management trust, the affiliating university, and the state government.
Rules, regulations, instructions, manuals, and records held or used Rules and regulations promulgated by the state government, university, and statutory bodies like AICTE are followed by the college.
Directory of its officers and employees List of teaching, non-teaching, and library staff is available in the college prospectus every year.
Facilities available to citizens for obtaining information College records and libraries are open to students and staff. Members of the public may access them with prior permission of the Principal.
Public Information Officers Information Officer: Dr. Karthik Panicker, Head, Department of English.
Assistant Information Officer: Shri P. L. Nandagawali, Registrar.
First Appellate Officer: Dr. Vikas Dhomne, Principal.
