College Mission and Vision

Our Mission and Vision



Our Mission

Commitment to create a center of excellence in quality technical education of world repute, within easy economic reach of all, moulding young minds into global leaders, capable of meeting future challenges, and contributing towards making India a developed nation.

Our Vision

  • To create an educational environment for development of skills in Engineering, Architecture, Managerial Competence, and Creativity.
  • To prepare students for the needs of the industry, providing trained manpower for the formation of a knowledgeable society.
  • To cater to industry needs by providing the latest technical know-how through consultancy, industry-institute partnerships, personnel development, and R&D activities.
  • To facilitate upgradation of knowledge and skills of manpower through continuous H.R.D. programmes.
  • To propagate and promote collective wisdom with human values for national integration.

College Plans

The college plans to consolidate its position as a premier learning institution in the area, enhance the quality of its services and products and increase the offerings it makes to the community. In keeping with its vision and mission, the college has charted out a short-term perspective plan. Comments and suggestions on the plan are most welcome at principaljpmc@rediffmail.com.
