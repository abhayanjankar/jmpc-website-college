College Activities and Facilities

Academic Activities

In addition to regular theory and practical work, the students are also involved in various project-based assignments. There are regular guest lectures by renowned faculty from various engineering and technical institutions. Numerous academic events like seminars, presentations, software exhibitions, technical exhibitions, and educational visits are also organized throughout the year.

The college also encourages students to participate in academic and related events organized by other institutions.

Sports & Recreation

The college has a full-fledged sports department with playgrounds and facilities for cricket, volleyball, basketball, ball-badminton, badminton, table tennis, athletics, etc. The college also has a well-equipped modern gym for students.

We are proud to mention that the college has so far produced 03 Chatrapati Award winners, 04 International players, and 70 National players.

Training & Placement

The college has a separate training and placement cell, which has been promoting tie-ups with renowned industrial and corporate houses. The patron of the management, Shri Praful Patel, and the Vice President, Shri Mukesh Patel, both leading industrialists, take a keen personal interest in such collaborations.

The college has already initiated collaborations with companies like Sunflag Iron & Steel Co. Ltd, Bhandara, BSNL, Jet Airways, Bharathi Enterprises, Reliance, Airtel, and other leading companies.
