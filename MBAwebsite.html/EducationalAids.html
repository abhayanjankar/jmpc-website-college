College Facilities and Activities

Educational Aids

The department is equipped with computers, an updated library, slide projectors, epidiascopes, television, video, P.A. System, Photocopier, etc. Every classroom has a dedicated Over Head Projector. Gun Projector, video cassettes, and V.C.D. are other educational aids used.

Seminar / Conference Hall

The air-conditioned seminar/conference hall is one of the best in Central India, with a seating capacity of about 80. Seminars and group discussions are frequently held in the state-of-the-art seminar hall equipped with the latest electronic gadgets. Students are always welcome to arrange seminars, conferences, G.D. or invite eminent people from the management field.

Placement Activities

The placement cell continues its activities through placement brochures, visits to websites, etc. The institute has a tie-up with professional placement agencies.

Guidance and Special Assistance

The department helps to undertake small projects for various companies through student support. Seminars, G.D. are regular features. Study materials are also provided. Computer lab access is available to all students.

Alumni Association

Several alumni of the college are working at top management levels in big companies like HLL, MAHINDRA, TATA AIRWAYS, ASHOK LEYLAND, SUNFLAG, WIPRO, etc. The alumni are in constant touch with the department and also bring placement opportunities for the students of the department.

Other Activities

The department helps in organizing various extracurricular programs, inter-collegiate competitions, etc. by its students for developing leadership qualities among students.
