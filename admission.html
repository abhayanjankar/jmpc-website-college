College Admission Rules

Rules for Admission

Application Process:

  • Application for admission to any of the courses should be submitted in the prescribed form attached to the prospectus.
  • The form must be completely filled in and submitted so as to reach the office on or before the last date for registration of candidates.
  • Two true copies of the mark sheet of the degree examination.
  • True copy of the Transfer or College Leaving Certificate of the last institute attended.
  • True copy of S.S.C. Certificate for date of birth.
  • A copy of recent passport size photograph to be affixed at the proper place on the application form. One more copy of the photograph should be kept ready for the identity card in case of students getting admission.
  • Two self-addressed envelopes.
  • Charges for Prospectus / Application forms are not refundable.
  • Incomplete forms in any respect are liable to be rejected.

Selection Procedure:

  • Selection will be done on the basis of marks obtained at the degree examination and personal interview.
  • A merit list of the provisionally selected candidates will be displayed on the notice board of the college.
  • Provisionally selected candidates will have to submit documents as given below in the prospectus.
  • Admission will be finalized only after submission of original documents and payment of fees before the last date.

Documents to be Furnished:

  • Mark sheet of degree examination.
  • Transfer or Leaving Certificate of the last Institute attended. In no case will admission be finalized without the submission of this document.
  • Certificate of Eligibility from Nagpur University for candidates graduated from other Universities.
  • Original degree for verification.
  • Migration Certificate for those who come from other Universities.
