Library Infrastructure

The College has one of the best infrastructures in the region. A beautiful campus, neat, clean & spacious classrooms with good ventilation, and well-equipped laboratories make it an ideal place to learn.

We also have 2 SMART classrooms, 4 Computer Labs, and 1 Language-lab. In addition to the Central Computer Lab with 120 computers, we provide seamless learning opportunities. Water-coolers connected to the RO facility ensure that our students get hygienic water year-round.

The College also has a state-of-the-art fully air-conditioned Conference Hall – Pearl Chamber – with a seating capacity of 300 persons.

Library Details

The College has a well-stocked and fully computerised library with more than 70,000 books related to different disciplines taught in the college. There is a separate section for books related to competitive exams, and the library subscribes to about 50 Journals and Periodicals regularly.

The LIBMAN WEB-OPAC facility is available. The Library has a “Book Bank” consisting of core texts books, which are issued to deserving students for the whole academic year. The library is complemented by a spacious and well-lit Reading Room. The Reprographic section provides photocopying facilities to the students and staff. N-LIST & INFLIBNET provide unlimited access to e-learning resources.
