Introduction to IQAC

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) plays a pivotal role in ensuring the quality of academic and administrative activities of the institution. Its primary goal is to foster a quality culture in every aspect of the institution.

Objectives of IQAC

  • To develop a quality system for conscious, consistent, and catalytic action to improve the academic and administrative performance of the institution.
  • To promote measures for institutional functioning towards quality enhancement through the internalization of quality culture and institutionalization of best practices.
