About NSS

The NSS unit at J.M. Patel College consists of 150 active members who participate in a wide range of year-round programmes. These include:

  • Awareness rallies, campaigns, blood donation, tree plantation
  • National days and anniversaries celebrations
  • Debates, elocutions, cultural programmes, street plays
  • State, university, and district-level camps
  • Special training for disaster management, personality development, nature conservation
  • Village adoption for development

Special Achievements

The NSS unit has won numerous accolades, including:

  • Best Unit awards at the university level
  • Best Volunteer awards at university, state, and district levels
  • Best Programme Officer awards
  • Active participation in RD Parades at national and state level
